what is a digital garden?

Digital gardens are a place where one can curate their creative pursuits, ponderances, and digital artifacts that warrant further exploration. Each person can decide how their garden grows. When reflecting on what a digital garden is... it can really be anything. Some differentiate it from a blog, and it may blossom into something beyond a wiki. As digital gardeners, we collect seeds throughout our exploration of cyberspace. I draw inspiration from others who have planted digital gardens before me.
